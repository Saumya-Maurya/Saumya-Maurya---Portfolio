AI Thermal Cameras

Real-Time Public Health and Safety Monitoring

Project Overview

The Challenge: Ensuring public health and safety in crowded environments, especially during health crises, has become a critical priority. Traditional systems often struggle to provide real-time insights, leaving critical gaps in monitoring and response. This project aims to address these challenges with an AI-powered thermal camera system.

Our Solution: The AI Thermal Camera system integrates advanced thermal imaging technology and machine learning algorithms to provide a real-time solution for detecting elevated body temperatures and monitoring crowd density. This system allows for dynamic health advisories, real-time alerts, and advanced crowd management in environments such as airports, hospitals, and large public venues.

Key Benefits: By automating temperature checks, monitoring crowd flow, and providing actionable insights, this system ensures better public health management, enhances safety, and prevents overcrowding. It plays a vital role in high-traffic environments and critical facilities during emergencies, such as the COVID-19 pandemic.

Technologies Used

  • Python: The core programming language for real-time data processing and implementing system logic.
  • YOLOv5: AI model used for real-time people detection and tracking in dynamic environments.
  • SORT: An algorithm used to track and manage multiple individuals detected across video frames.
  • Tesseract OCR: Optical Character Recognition technology used for extracting temperature data from thermal video feeds.

Project Features

  • Real-Time People Detection & Tracking: Accurately tracks individuals in high-density crowds for health monitoring and safety management using YOLOv5 and SORT.
  • Temperature Monitoring and Alerts: Automatically detects elevated body temperatures and triggers real-time alerts, helping identify health risks such as fever.
  • Dynamic Health Messaging: Displays health advisories and safety instructions on digital signboards based on real-time temperature and crowd data, improving public safety.
  • Advanced Occupancy Management: Monitors crowd density in real-time and dynamically adjusts occupancy limits to prevent overcrowding in sensitive environments like hospitals, airports, and event venues.
